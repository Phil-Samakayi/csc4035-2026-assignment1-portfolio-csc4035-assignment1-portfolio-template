About Me

Hi! I am Phil SAMAKAYI, an aspiring Web Developer based in Lusaka, Zambia, currently completing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering major at the University of Zambia. I'm passionate about building responsive, user friendly software that solves real world problems. my love for tech was bread from both my urban and suburb experiences, enabling me to be fascinated by the role of technology not only in connecting people and businesses but also in the transformative power that it has which has inspired me to be a part of. With strong curiosity and love for experimentation, my goals include contributing to open source projects and landing a junior developer role and eventually specializing in full-stack Development with Java Script frameworks. When not working on school projects, I enjoy coding or working on my individual projects, reading about emerging technologies and novels. I am excited to collaborate and bring innovative ideas to life!
